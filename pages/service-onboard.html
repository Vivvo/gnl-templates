Purpose and Objective

  1. The Public Library is committed to providing a quality environment and universal access for the delivery of information for cultural, economic, educational and recreational development to the public. The Public Library's purpose and objective in enacting the within bylaw is to maintain a safe environment for all staff and patrons of the Public Library, and further, to protect and preserve property under its control. SHORT TITLE
  2. This bylaw may be cited as: "The Public Library Safe Use and Conduct Bylaw".

Conduct of Patrons

  1. Patrons shall use all collections, equipment, facilities and furniture on the premises of the Public Library for their intended purposes.
  2. Patrons shall abide by all Public Library Regulations.
  3. Upon registration for a permanent Regina Public Library card, patrons shall present two pieces of current identification: one of which establishes a Regina residency; and the other a valid Saskatchewan Health Card or other identification approved by the Public Library.
  4. The Public Library is a smoke free environment. Smoking is prohibited in all areas within the Library including the lobby.
    1. Consumption of non-alcoholic beverages and eating is allowed only in designated areas within the Public Library;
    2. Patrons may consume alcoholic beverages only:
      1. in designated areas; and
      2. when a liquor license for library purposes has been obtained.
  6. Except for guide dogs, patrons shall not bring animals into the Public Library.
  7. The Public Library reserves the right to require children on library premises to be accompanied by an adult.
  8. All patrons of the Public Library shall comply with applicable public health codes and regulations.
